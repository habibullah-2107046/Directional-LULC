## Temporal Directional Analysis of Land Use Land Cover (2000–2025)

This map illustrates the **temporal directional expansion of Land Use Land Cover (LULC)** in the Rajshahi Development Authority (RDA) area from **2000 to 2025**.

The directional analysis was conducted to identify the **dominant spatial directions of land use transformation**, helping to understand how urbanization, agriculture, and other land categories expanded over time.

### Method Overview
- Multi-temporal **LULC classification maps** were generated for the years **2000, 2005, 2010, 2015, 2020, and 2025**.
- The study area was divided into **directional sectors (N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW)**.
- Each sector was analyzed to determine the **distribution and expansion of different land use categories**.

### Land Use Categories
- Agricultural Land  
- Commercial Area  
- Residential Area  
- Industrial Area  
- Vegetation  
- Bare Land  
- Water Body  

This approach helps visualize **spatial growth patterns and land use transitions**, providing insights for **urban planning and environmental management**.
